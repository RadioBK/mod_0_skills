CLASS: FOOD

ATTRIBUTES: Temperature (Integer)
            Vegetarian (Boolean)
            Weight (Float)
            Dish (String)

METHODS: add_animal_product (affect the vegetarian attribute - true or false)
         change_dish (change the Dish attribute - specific foods are plated on certain dish types. bowl/plate/platter)
         add_heat (change the temperature attribute. Potentially in only one direction)
         eat_food (this would affect the weight attribute)
